About Me

Hi, I’m Beyonca Ademulegun, a Communication and Integrated Digital Marketing student at UMass Amherst with a deep passion for strategic communication, merchandising, and visual storytelling. I thrive at the intersection of creativity and data, whether I’m forecasting fashion trends, crafting digital content, or building campaigns that make an impact. Over the past few years, I’ve gained hands-on experience through internships at Macy’s, Shell, and the UMass President’s Office, working on everything from product performance analysis and internal communications to brand-aligned promotions and campaign design. I also had the incredible opportunity to study abroad at the University of Westminster in London, where I expanded my global perspective and creative toolkit. This website was hand-coded by me using HTML, reflecting not only my technical skills but also my love for design and detail. It serves as a home for my projects, work samples, and the journey I’m on as a young professional in marketing, merchandising, and communications. Thanks for stopping by! I hope you find something here that inspires you. Let’s connect!
